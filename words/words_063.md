# Adda

adda | ˈəˌdä | noun Indian English 1 a place where people gather for conversation. • an illicit drinking place: the young men spent their time at the street-corner addas and tea stalls. • informal conversation among a group of people: “Go upstairs and enjoy a little adda with our neighbors.”. 2 a junction point for public transport. ORIGIN from Hindi aḍḍā, originally in the sense ‘perch for tame birds’.
