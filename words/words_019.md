# absentee

absentee ab·sen·tee | ˌabsənˈtē | noun a person who is expected or required to be present at a place or event but is not: an absentee from the match | [as modifier] : absentee pupils. • [as modifier] US English denoting or relating to a system of voting in which voters who are unable to be present at the polls complete their ballots and typically submit them by mail in advance of an election: election officials are predicting absentee voting will become even more popular in the future | they vouched for new policies that would require absentee voters to submit photo identification.

# absenteeism

absenteeism ab·sen·tee·ism | ˌabsənˈtēˌizəm | noun the practice of regularly staying away from work or school without good reason: high levels of absenteeism caused by low job motivation.

# absinthe
